JOB INFORMATION

To join Hot Beans Web, there are some requirements:

  • You must know how to write code in at least 1 programming language (Python, C#, etc)
  • Create well designed websites and applications (placement of elements such as buttons or colour)
  • Communicate and work with a group of individuals
  • Test applications and features
  • Pass atleast 3 GCSEs and computer science related subjects if possible

    • These requirements are crucial to become a part of our team as a web developer.

    Therefore, they are needed to perform assigned tasks depending on your career.

    To see where or how you can learn to meet these requirements, click COURSES.

    CAREERS

    Front-End Developer

    Duties

    Create user interfaces that are good quality and enjoyable for other users. You must communicate with other employees such as website designers to see what should do what and turn it into reality whilst considering optimisation and performance on certain devices.

    Salary

    £40,000 - £87,500 per year

    Back-End Developer

    Duties

    Create code that allows the website to communicate to databases or servers which allows for applications/surveys and navigating or searching through the website.You must ensure that the website works correctly without any bugs or issues.

    Salary

    £48,079 - £68,000 per year

    Web Designer

    Duties

    Design how websites look and perform. The layout and elements must be sorted and placed in good areas and should complement the colours chosen and should prevent users from getting confused.

    Salary

    £27,003 - £61,612 per year
